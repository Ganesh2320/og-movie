About the Movie

OG - Original Gangsters is an upcoming action drama film about a ruthless don named Ojas Gambheera "OG" who returns to Mumbai after a decade to kill Omi Bhau, a crime boss. The film is directed by Sujeeth and stars Pawan Kalyan, Priyanka Arul Mohan, Arjun Das, and Emraan Hashmi. It is scheduled for release in theaters on September 27, 2024 in Telugu, Hindi, Tamil, Kannada, and Malayalam.
